Member Services Representative — Northfield Credit Union

About Us
Northfield Credit Union is a community-focused financial cooperative serving members across the Western Region. We've been providing personal banking, loans, and financial education to our community for over 60 years. Our members are our owners, and every interaction matters.

Role
We are looking for a Member Services Representative to join our branch team. You'll be the first point of contact for members, handling everyday transactions, answering questions about accounts and services, and building lasting relationships with the people we serve.

Responsibilities
- Process member transactions accurately: deposits, withdrawals, transfers, loan payments, and account inquiries
- Open new accounts and assist members with product selection based on their financial needs
- Maintain accurate records and adhere to all compliance and confidentiality requirements
- Identify opportunities to refer members to financial advisors or additional services
- Support branch operations including cash drawer management and daily balancing
- Respond to member concerns with professionalism and empathy

Requirements
- 1+ years of customer service or cash handling experience
- Strong attention to detail and accuracy with numbers
- Comfortable learning financial software and CRM systems
- Excellent written and verbal communication
- Ability to build rapport quickly and maintain professionalism under pressure
- Commitment to member privacy and regulatory compliance

Nice to Have
- Experience in a banking or credit union environment
- Knowledge of FINTRAC compliance requirements
